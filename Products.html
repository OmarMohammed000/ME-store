The hieroglyphic alphabet

It refers to the writing system used in ancient Egypt to write hieroglyphic texts. It consisted of a combination of ideographic, phonetic, and determinative signs. While there is no direct Egyptian equivalent to the modern alphabet, there were hieroglyphic signs that corresponded to certain sounds.
