Key of Life

The "Key of Life" is a symbol that originates from ancient Egyptian mythology and religious beliefs. It is also known as the Ankh, which resembles a looped cross with a rounded top. The Ankh is often depicted being carried by gods and goddesses in ancient Egyptian art.

Tutankhamun Mask

Tutankhamun Mask, also known as the Death Mask of Tutankhamun, is one of the most iconic and recognizable artifacts from ancient Egypt.It depicts the face of the young pharaoh and is adorned with precious gemstones and colored glass inlays. The mask was meant to protect and preserve the pharaoh's face in the afterlife and is considered a masterpiece of ancient Egyptian funerary art.
